French Toast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp, milk
  • 1/4th tsp, cinnamon powder
  • 1 tsp, butter
  • 2 whole wheat bread slices
  • 1 tsp Sliced Raisins

Cooking Instructions

Make a mixture of eggs, cinnamon powder, sliced raisins and milk. Heat the saut√© pan and add the butter. Dip bread in the mixture soaking both sides. Place the slice on the pan and cook both sides until golden. Serve the toast. Taste best with plum sauce or maple syrup.
